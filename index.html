Meet Our CEO

MR GODSON HENRY is the CEO of GIPBSON HOMES LIMITED, a home of real estate excellence. He is an entrepreneur who has put so much into entrepreneurship over the past few years of his life. Born in 1983 in Ubulu-Uku town in Aniocha South LGA of Delta State, Nigeria, he believes in wealth creation through passive and multiple incomes. He grew up surrounded by entrepreneurs who inspired his passion for business and excellence.

Mr. Henry founded Gipbson Homes Limited out of a deep passion for entrepreneurship and value creation. His vision is simple: deliver excellence and value to all stakeholders — investors, contractors, and employees — ensuring that everyone benefits from the company’s success.
